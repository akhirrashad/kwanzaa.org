ABOUT THIS WEBSITE

Kwanzaa

Welcome to Kwanzaa.org. This website exists to celebrate and advance Kwanzaa and the Nguzo Saba (The Seven Principles) during the Kwanzaa holiday season and all year round. To make that possible, this site will be a living example of the applied principles in action. We practice Umoja (Unity), Kujichagulia (Self Determination), and Ujima (Collective Work and Responsibility) in our coming together to create this project. We will be practicing Ujamaa (Cooperative Economics) thru this website, and have been designated as an "Approved Kwanzaa Products" supplier as linked on the Official Kwanzaa Website (officialkwanzaawebsite.org/approvedproducts.html). Our chosen Nia (Purpose) is to build on our own Kuumba (Creativity) and Imani (Faith) in ourselves and our people to create a website that celebrates and advances Kwanzaa and the Nguzo Saba (The Seven Principles) as a living vocation, in the most meaningful way, all year long.
